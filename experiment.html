Experiment

What we did：

After designing the DNA single strand sequence, we set annealing and hybridization temperatures using a PCR instrument to self connect the DNA single strand to form assembly components, which were then hybridized to form a hybrid structure. We also demonstrated the formation of the desired structure through some methods.

Methods


  • DNA self connection
    1. System: 20uL
    2. ssDNA（50uM）16uL
    3. ATP(10mM) 0.48uL
    4. 10×PNK Buffer 2uL
    5. H2O 0.52 uL
    6. PNK enzyme (10U/uL) 1 uL
    7. After reacting overnight at 37℃, terminate the reaction at 75℃ for 15 minutes

  • Restriction enzyme digestion
    1. Take 10ul of self connecting product and add:
    2. Exo1 0.3ul
    3. Exo3 0.5ul
    4. Exo1 buffer 0.5ul
    5. Exo3 buffer 0.5ul
    6. Reaction for 4 hours.

  • Hybridization of dumbbell structure
    1. System: 10uL
    2. Self connecting product1 2uL
    3. Self connecting product2 2uL
    4. Mgcl2 (100mM) 1uL
    5. Hepes buffer (Ph7.5 100mM) 1uL
    6. H2O 4uL
    7. Hybridization time: 95 ℃ for 3 minutes, cooled to 37 ℃ at 0.1 ℃/s, overnight (10 hours).

  • Detection of z-dna formation using ADAR1
    1. System:5ul
    2. Add ADAR1 protein according to
    3. ADAR1 protein: DNA dumbbell concentration=0
    4. ADAR1 protein: DNA dumbbell concentration=2.5
    5. ADAR1 protein: DNA dumbbell concentration=10
    6. ADAR1 protein: DNA dumbbell concentration=20
