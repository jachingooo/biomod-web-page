Simulation

Welcome to our world! Now you will experience the complete storage process in which we divide it into 3 steps.

  • Step one: Encoding

    • Entering Chinese characters in our software, you will obtain the corresponding DNA sequences.


    step 1. Encoding. What's inside the box on the red line is the Chinese characters to be stored, and what's inside the box under the red line is the DNA sequences needed to store these Chinese characters.


  • Step two: Amplification

    • According to the sequence you have got, you can synthesize DNA and then do some experiments. During the experiments, the changes of DNA are shown in the animation below. Now you can preserve the DNA to store the information.

    step 2. Amplification


  • Step three: Decoding

    • After using nanopore sequencing to read the DNA base sequences, we can get some base sequences. Our software will obtain the reverse complementary information of the sequence, select, sort, and screen the DNA fragment with the highest repetition rate. And then it can be translated into the original information through our software.

    step 3-2. All the steps for retrieve information, as shown in the red box.



    step 3-3. Decoding. What's inside the box on the red line is the DNA sequence, and what's inside the box under the red line is the Chinese characters we stored.
